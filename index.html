The findings suggest that previous estimates of dinosaur sizes and their locomotion could be inaccurate, potentially altering our understanding of these prehistoric creatures. The implications of this research could lead to a reevaluation of dinosaur biology and behavior, prompting further investigation into the methodologies used in paleontological studies.
